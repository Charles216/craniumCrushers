Campaign Query

Campaign Query is the brainchild of Charlie Grogan, Amy Moretti, Joe Plukarski, and Bobbi Rose. After realizing how difficult it was to find and sort through campaign finance data, we were inspired to create something straightforward and visually appealling. Using API information from the Federal Election Commission and data sets available from the Center for Responsible Politics, we chose to highlight donations and contributions to federal elections by various interest groups and individual donors.

Campaign Query is a full-stack app using a variety of technologies including Javascript and jQuery, Node.js, Express.js, MySQL and Sequelize, as well as Amcharts for displaying the data.
